Greatest Movies According to Miranda

Top 3 Must Watch Movies

Pride and Prejudice

This is the classic tale of Elizabeth Bennett and Mr. Darcy by Jane Austin.
Beautifully performed by Keira Knightly and Colin Firth.

Ever After

Age old tale of Cinderella, performed by Drew Barrymore.
Excellent scenery, costume design and performance.

Cast Away

Tom Hanks' performance was gripping in this drama.
